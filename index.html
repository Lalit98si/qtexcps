Your Trusted Partner in Quality Assurance

QTex CPS is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company and an ISO/IEC 17025:2017 Accredited Calibration Lab, established in 2014. We specialize in supplying Testing & Quality Control Instruments and providing calibration services to a wide range of industries. Our expertise lies in setting up Quality Control Labs for Commercial Labs, Garment Exporters, Buying Houses, Spinning & Weaving Mills, Dyeing & Processing Houses, and more.

At QTex CPS, we are committed to upholding international quality standards and delivering excellence. Our dedicated team of highly qualified engineers ensures that our products and services meet globally recognized standards and specifications, including AATCC, ISO, BIS, ASTM, and others.
